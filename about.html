About

I’m an application security professional and vulnerability researcher with experience spanning healthcare technology, penetration testing consulting, secure software development, and offensive security research.

My career began at Epic Systems, working closely with healthcare organizations to deploy, integrate, and maintain critical healthcare infrastructure — a foundation in large-scale enterprise systems, operational security, and the challenges of securing technology in highly regulated environments.

I later joined WhiteHat Security (now Synopsys), where I spent more than three years performing over 300 web application security assessments across a diverse range of industries. That work sharpened my ability to rapidly understand complex applications, identify weaknesses, and communicate risk effectively to engineering teams.

Most recently, I spent four years at Audible focused on Application Security — partnering with engineering teams to build and scale secure development practices while helping secure products used by millions of customers.

Today I’m pursuing vulnerability research and bug bounty hunting full-time. My work focuses on discovering high-impact vulnerabilities in modern web applications and cloud environments, while exploring how AI-powered tools and autonomous agents can augment and scale security research.

Outside of security, I play brass instruments and enjoy writing about interesting security problems and emerging trends in the industry.

  • AI-augmented workflows for offensive security research
  • Open-source library and modern web application vulnerabilities
